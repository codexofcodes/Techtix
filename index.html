About Us

Techtix, Asia's largest college cultural festival, has developed over the years into so much more: a melting pot of Indian and global culture, a launchpad for upcoming artists, a spectacular showcase of talent, an epitome of incredible human experience: where you can never see it all, but what you do see lasts a lifetime, where friendships are born and memories are made amidst the gleam of several bonfires as the nights intensify Our mission is to bring along all of you and invigorate you with extreme amounts of pure happiness and ecstacy, and also serve as an escape from all the bitterness in our lives. This is what drives us into our 49th edition this year! Embrace yourself for another cultural extravaganza: join us this year at Techtix!

Events

Whiplash

Need a break from all the competition? Want to just sit back and listen to some music? Well, here’s Whiplash! Listen to some of the best singers from the many bands performing in this year’s UTSAV! Wave along the crowd, listening to your favourite tunes and sing your worries away!


Fashion Show

Have what it takes to walk the talk? Well, prove us right in BMSCE UTSAV’s Fashion Show! Bigger, Better and Sassier than before, bring your models and showcase your best looks. May the best team win.


EDM Night

Feel every rhythm and every beat flow through your veins and jump even harder when the beat drops at the EDM night of this year’s UTSAV. Get ready to have your world rocked. See you there!


Sahas

Hope you’re not scared of heights! Show off your bravery and climbing skills at our Wall Climbing Events, both competitive and non-competitive. Be Daring. Be Vigilant. And most important of all, Have Fun!


FAQ

Why registration is necessary?

Registering on the website will generate a unique MI number for you. This MI number is essential for participation or entry to all the activities going on during Mood Indigo! If you want to register for a competition or accommodation or collect concert passes against your college ID, you’ll need to have an MI number. Your MI number is the key to the best things the festival has to offer.

How do I register for Techtix?

Go to the Register tab to get your MI number.

I cannot find my college in the drop-down list. What should I do?

If you do not find your college on the list, then submit the form using “Not Found” in the college tab.

Is the entry free?

The entry has been and will always be free. All you need is a college ID!
